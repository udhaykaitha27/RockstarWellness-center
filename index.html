About the Founder

Dr. Yamarthi Sai Durga Prasad

Dr. Yamarthi Sai Durga Prasad is the Founder and Chairman of Rockstar Wellness Center, a dedicated wellness educator, social reformer and advocate for human rights and quality education. With a strong commitment to empowering individuals through health awareness, skill development and professional education, he has inspired many people to achieve personal and professional growth.

Dr. Yamarthi Sai Durga Prasad has been honored with an Honorary Doctorate in recognition of his outstanding contributions to wellness education, community service and social development. He is also an Iconic Human Rights and Educational Orientation Award Achiever, reflecting his dedication to promoting education, equality, leadership and social responsibility.

Through Rockstar Wellness Center, he works to create opportunities in wellness, alternative medicine, paramedical education and career-oriented training programs. His vision is to build a healthier, more knowledgeable and self-reliant society.
